### About

[Self Care Catalysts](http://www.selfcarecatalysts.com/) is a health solutions company powered by patient intelligence and analytics to promote positive health outcomes. We offer a mobile and desktop platform known as Health Storylines, which offers a patient-centered, self-care solution.
